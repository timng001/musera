Welcome to the About Us page!

Musera was founded on September 18th, 2020 to provide an immersive and meaningful listening experience to all the hits spanning from the 1950s to the present day. Utilizing a user-friendly interface and weekly Billboard Hot 100 records, Musera provides access to an extensive library of chart-topping music. Furthermore, Musera's ambient colors adapt to the valence of the songs, giving a feel-good visual supplement to the streaming platform.
